KERALA TOURISM

Kerala Tourism is the official promotion and development arm of tourism in the Indian state of Kerala, often referred to as “Gods" Own Country. It is one of the most popular and well-organized tourism departments in India and has earned global recognition for its initiatives in sustainable and eco-friendly tourism.

  • Backwaters: A unique network of lagoons, lakes, and canals – most famous in Alleppey and Kumarakom.
  • Beaches: Kovalam, Varkala, Marari, and Bekal offer beautiful shorelines.
  • Hill Stations: Munnar, Wayanad, and Thekkady are known for tea plantations, cool weather, and wildlife.
  • Waterfalls:Kerala is enrich with number of waterfalls..Especially Athirapally waterfalls
