Thiruvananthapuram, also known as Trivandrum, is the capital city of Kerala, India. It's known as the "Evergreen City of India" and is characterized by its greenery, rolling hills, and coastal location. The city is a blend of tradition and modernity, with bustling markets, vibrant festivals, and a thriving arts scene.

  • Geography: Situated on the west coast, with the Arabian Sea to the west and the Western Ghats to the east.
  • Climate: The city enjoys a tropical climate and is known for its pleasant weather, especially during the winter months.
  • Culture: It's a city of temples, museums, and cultural institutions, with a rich heritage of art and culture.
  • Tourism: Popular attractions include the Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Kovalam beach, Shanghumugham beach, and the Napier Museum.
